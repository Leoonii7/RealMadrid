Real Madrid History

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Founded in 1902, the club has a rich and storied history.

Real Madrid is one of the most successful football clubs in the world, known for its achievements in both domestic and international competitions. The club has a passionate fan base and a historic rivalry with FC Barcelona, known as El Clásico.

Some of the club's legendary players include Cristiano Ronaldo, Alfredo Di Stéfano, and Zinedine Zidane. Real Madrid's iconic stadium, Santiago Bernabéu, is a symbol of the club's history and tradition.
